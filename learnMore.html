Meet Some Teachers

Reginaldo Simões

Teacher in the field of Humanities. Has experience in the field of Education, with an emphasis on Adult Education, primarily focusing on the following theme: society, work, and culture;

Holds degrees in three areas of Humanities and Social Sciences: History, Geography, and Philosophy.


Thiago Batista Moreira

EBTT Teacher at the Federal Institute of Amazonas, stationed at the Manacapuru Advanced Campus. Has expertise in the field of Metabolism, Biochemistry, and Plant Physiology, as well as in Metabolic Modeling and Microalgae;

Holds a degree in Biological Sciences, with a Master's and Doctorate in Botany from the University of Brasília.


Lorenzo Barroco

Has a Bachelor's degree in Fisheries Engineering and a Master's degree in Tropical Fisheries Sciences, both from UFAM;

Holds a Ph.D. in Animal Science and Fisheries Resources from UFAM, in partnership with the Geochronology Laboratory of the University of Brasília and BOREA, from the National Museum of Natural History in Paris, France (2019).
